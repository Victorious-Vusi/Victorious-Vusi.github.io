THE MINIMALIST MAGICIAN

Exploring simplicity through code

Hello, I’m Surplus Notion, a web developer based in South Africa. Since a young age, I’ve been overflowing with creativity, and my passion for simplicity knows no bounds. My journey into web development began during high school amidst the 2020 Covid-19 pandemic. It didn’t take long for me to realize that this realm was the perfect canvas to channel my creative energy and minimalist ideals. And so, my magical journey commenced.

What I do?

As a web developer, I specialize in designing and developing websites for small businesses and individuals. My approach is rooted in minimalism, where simplicity meets elegance. I create clean, uncluttered interfaces with subtle yet captivating animations. As a freelancer, I take pride in crafting websites that stand out from the crowd—each project a unique expression of my design philosophy. Feel free to explore my portfolio of completed works to immerse yourself in my distinctive design language.
